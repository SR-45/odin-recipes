Pizza

Description

A standard recipe for pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • parmesan

Steps

  1. Pour flour into bowl and add water
  2. Mix and leave for 1 hour
  3. Mix with dissolved yeast and cover with cling film
  4. Heat grill and put dough on grill
  5. Pour sauce and toppings
  6. Heat pan and cook pizza for 2 minutes
  7. Grill for 2 minutes
