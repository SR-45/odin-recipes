Lasagna

Description

A standard recipe for lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make ricotta
  4. Layer lasagna as you like
  5. Cover with foil, bake
  6. Let the lasagna cool
  7. Eat!
